Company’s brief

CANBOT To be the mover and shaker of intelligent service robots

CANBOT is a leading intellectual service robotics creator ,manufacturer and commercial solutions provider,engaged in providing most creative robotic products and solutions for both family and industrial customers. As one of China’s high-tech enterprises,CANBOT gains investments from public companies and financial organizations such as Canny Elevator Co. UNIS,SES and Sensegain asset management etc. .

CANBOT entered intellectual robots reserch field since 2008 and becomes expert in AI ,voice recognition,indoor navigation,smart sensor, interactive softwears, while coopertating with top reserch organizations like CSIST,INTEL,IBM,TSINGHUA,HUAWEI,NVIDIA .
With supports from top engineering and technology reserch teams in the world,CANBOT has complete production lines covering humanoid commercial use robots, parter type intellgent home service robot, education&entertainment robot,table size cute robot..
Thanks to our own one hundred protected patents,CANBOT is the key member of China Service Robotic Standardization and the member of China Robotics Testing and Certification alliance.
CANBOT is the first ISO9001 certified service robotics enterprise in China and is elected to be CHINA TOP10 robots in both 2016,2017 by Ministry of Industry and Information China.

CANBOT flagship intellectual U robots,gained the widest family service robots applied cases so far, which are the classics in service robots industry. In 2015, the launch of U-partner humanoid robots represent an epoch-making benchmarking event.
Crossover collaboration of business model,intensive reserch and development of AI, CANBOT created one after another unbelivable intellectual service robots industry miracles.The leading business model, perfect product creativity,top R&D team,experienced marketing networks, these make the CANBOT to be mover and shaker in this industry.
